Fox

DESCRIPTION
Foxes are small to medium-sized omnivorous mammals belonging to the family Canidae. They are known for their cunning nature and adaptability to various environments, from forests and grasslands to urban areas. The most common species is the red fox (Vulpes vulpes), which has a distinctive reddish coat, bushy tail, and sharp features. Foxes are solitary hunters, primarily feeding on small mammals, birds, insects, and fruits. They are also known for their playful behavior and complex vocalizations, which they use to communicate with each other.
